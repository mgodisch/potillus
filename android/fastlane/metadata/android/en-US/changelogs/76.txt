Removes the app's separate database encryption layer (SQLCipher); your data is now protected at rest by Android's storage encryption and the app sandbox. It still never leaves the device on its own — move it between phones only via Backup → Export/Import, now explained step by step in the guide. Note: data from earlier versions isn't migrated automatically, so export a backup before updating and re-import it afterwards. Also adds optional release signing and Play upload tooling.
