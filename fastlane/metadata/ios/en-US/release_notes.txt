This version brings the app in line with its Android counterpart and fixes several problems found in a full review. A fresh installation now creates the built-in preset drinks, so the drinks list is no longer empty at first launch, and importing a backup with "Replace" now really replaces the catalogue. The app lock engages immediately on a cold start, and the screens follow the clock: the blood-alcohol estimate updates every minute, and Today, Statistics and Calendar move to the new day on their own. Backup and import messages and the Face ID prompt appear in the selected language, and the in-app guide and license text read as proper paragraphs. Today, Calendar, Drinks, Statistics and Settings were reworked to match the Android layout, wording and colours; the language list offers "(System)" for following the device language; "About" moved out of Settings into the menu; and the app icon is larger and crisper.
