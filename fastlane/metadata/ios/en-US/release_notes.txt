This release refreshes the app’s look and fixes several issues.

Reliability
• The app lock now engages immediately on a cold start, so your diary is protected from the very first launch.
• Screens no longer freeze when the app returns from the background, and all views now follow the current date correctly.

Look and feel
• The Today, Calendar, Drinks, Statistics and Settings screens were reworked to match the layout, wording and colours — the same information now sits in the same place.
• The statistics screen shows your key figures in two clear cards, marks days over a limit in red and abstinent days in green, and draws a green check-mark for every dry day on the consumption chart.
• The calendar day view gains a daily-limit bar, richer entry rows and edit and delete actions.

Language and about
• You can now set the app language to “(System)” to follow your device language, and switch back to it any time under Settings.
• The overflow menu now offers an “About” screen with the version, the app’s licence and its open-source components, linking to the full copyright and licence text.

Fixes
• Backup and import messages now appear in the app’s language.
• The in-app guide and licence text now read as clean paragraphs.
• The statistics start date can always be changed again.
