This version brings the app in line with its Android counterpart and fixes several problems found in a full review. A fresh installation now creates the built-in preset drinks, so the drinks list is no longer empty at first launch, and importing a backup with "Replace" now really replaces the catalogue. Statistics now begin on the day you installed the app, so the days before it are no longer counted as alcohol-free days. The app lock engages immediately on a cold start, and the screens follow the clock: the blood-alcohol estimate updates every minute, and Today, Statistics and Calendar move to the new day on their own. Backup and import messages and the Face ID prompt appear in the selected language, and the in-app guide and license text read as proper paragraphs. The Calendar can now log a drink onto the day you have selected, and an entry on the Today screen can be edited or deleted from the row itself. On the Statistics screen the consumption chart now comes first, and the drink categories are drawn as a donut with their grams and shares beside it. Today, Calendar, Drinks, Statistics and Settings were reworked to match the Android layout, wording and colours; the language list offers "(System)" for following the device language; "About" moved out of Settings into the menu; and the app icon is larger and crisper.
