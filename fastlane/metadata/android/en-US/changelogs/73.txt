The statistics screen now opens on the monthly view by default. A new Security
section in Settings lets you allow screenshots and screen recordings, which are
blocked by default to protect your data. The Today screen now shows today's
total in grams next to the month's average, with the trend arrow in bold. The
PDF report adds your longest abstinence streak, highlights the max-per-day and
max-per-7-days figures in red when over the limit, and labels each trend bar
with its value.
